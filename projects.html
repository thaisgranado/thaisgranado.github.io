Impact of Artificial Intelligence on the Job Market

This project, developed at Reading College, explores how artificial intelligence is reshaping employment structures, task distribution, and workforce dynamics.

The analysis focuses on how automation affects routine tasks, the increasing importance of human judgement in complex decision-making, and the need for continuous adaptation in a rapidly evolving labour market.

Key insights include the growing relevance of human oversight in AI-driven systems, the risks associated with over-reliance on automation, and the importance of governance frameworks to ensure responsible implementation.

This work reflects my broader interest in structured evaluation, decision-making, and the intersection between human judgement and intelligent systems.
