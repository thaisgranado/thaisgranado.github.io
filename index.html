Why AI Evaluation

My interest in AI comes from its reliance on human judgement, data quality, and evaluation processes. AI systems are only as useful as the quality of the data, decisions, and review processes that support them.

I am particularly interested in how human reasoning interacts with machine-generated outputs: evaluating relevance, checking consistency, identifying errors, and ensuring that systems remain reliable, safe, and aligned with real-world use.

Rather than approaching AI only as a technical topic, I approach it through the lenses of governance, accountability, documentation, and structured human judgement.
