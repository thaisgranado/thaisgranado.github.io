About Me

I have a background in Law and began my career within the Brazilian judicial system, working with legal documentation, case analysis, and later as a certified mediator and conciliator.

This experience required structured thinking, neutrality, and responsibility, strengthening my ability to analyse information, identify inconsistencies, and make decisions in sensitive environments.

After moving to the United Kingdom, I expanded into corporate, administrative and customer-facing roles, developing adaptability, communication and organisational skills in fast-paced environments.

Alongside my professional experience, I have been continuously developing my knowledge through studies in corporate governance, data protection, business operations, Python, and artificial intelligence.

This portfolio reflects my journey across law, corporate environments, governance, and structured evaluation.
